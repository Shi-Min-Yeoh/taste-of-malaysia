About

Malay food is a vibrant and diverse cuisine that is shaped by the rich cultural heritage of Malaysia's Malay ethnic group. Known for its bold and aromatic flavors, Malay cuisine features a range of dishes that are often centered around fragrant spices and herbs, such as lemongrass, turmeric, and ginger. Malay food also places a strong emphasis on seafood and meat dishes, as well as vegetable and rice-based dishes. Some popular Malay dishes include nasi lemak, rendang, satay, and laksa. Overall, Malay food is a delicious and unique culinary experience that celebrates the cultural richness of Malaysia.

Kari ikan

Kari ikan is a delicious fish curry originating from Malaysia. It is typically made with a combination of fish that is cut into pieces, coconut milk, and a variety of spices such as fenugreek, cumin, curry leaves, chili peppers, mustard seeds, tamarind, ginger, turmeric, coriander, and lemongrass.
